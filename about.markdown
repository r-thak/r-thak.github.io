# About Me

I'm Rohan Thakkar, a Computer Science and Philosophy student at the University of Illinois Urbana-Champaign (Class of 2028). I'm passionate about systems programming, AI research, and improving education.

## What I'm Working On

Currently, I'm conducting undergraduate research with Dr. Frederic Sala, focusing on LLM distillation techniques for drone control. This work involves developing training environments using the Aerial Gym Simulator and verifying program correctness using Dafny.

I'm also interested in low-level systems, having worked on projects ranging from Minecraft anti-cheat systems to wireless connectivity solutions for medical devices.
